Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Please read the following to understand how we handle your data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Magnus Platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you use the Magnus Platform:

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, including your usage patterns, preferences, and device information.
  • Payment Information: If applicable, your payment details for processing transactions.
  • Referral Data: Data about users referred through your links and activities within the platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us to:

  • Provide and improve the Magnus Platform's features and services.
  • Process transactions and facilitate rewards.
  • Send you updates, promotional messages, and user support related to the platform.
  • Enhance security and prevent unauthorized access to your account.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. We use encryption technologies, secure servers, and regular monitoring to safeguard your information.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in delivering our services (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers).
  • To comply with legal requirements, such as subpoenas or court orders.
  • If we believe it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Magnus, our users, or the public.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in emails or by contacting support.
  • Request data portability for the information we have collected, in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Once the information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it in compliance with applicable laws.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
